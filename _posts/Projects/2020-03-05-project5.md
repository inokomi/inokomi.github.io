##### Overview

This game operates on the latest cryptocurrency technology and embraces a play-to-earn model, designed to simulate an economy. External testers representing various Filipino salary groups are given a set amount of dummy currency to engage with the game, helping researchers determine if it is possible for users to profit from playing. Each letter symbolizes a valuable crypto token, which players can seamlessly utilize, trade, forge, or even create. Calculation heavily relies on the <a href="https://www.investopedia.com/insights/what-is-the-quantity-theory-of-money/">Quantity Theory of Money.</a>

-----

##### The Game

The gameplay revolves around a passive approach, featuring a daily set of words. The rewards are determined by the letters, active NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) available within your team, combined with the passive NFT selected, granting you greater rewards based on the number of words you can form with them. In this intriguing game, the focus lies on strategic word selection and maximizing the potential of your letter combinations to achieve rewards. The game's main currency is called 'Ettr.'

-----

##### Forging

Players can increase stars and alter hashes and traits of both active and passive NFTs.

- NFTs can have a maximum of 4 stars and a minimum of 3 base parents.
- Hash changes and trait modifications are based on the hash values of the 3 base parents.
- Higher-star NFTs provide more rewards by amplifying the boosts they receive from their hashes.
- The forging system is designed to burn Ettr to maintain supply-demand balance.

-----

##### Marketplace

- Players can buy both active and passive NFTs directly from each other or from the Mint Market.
- In the Mint Market, profits are retained when players buy passive NFTs using SUSDC (USDC Coin).
- However, when players buy active NFTs, Ettr profits are burned, potentially changing on a production scale.

-----

##### Play

- The play menu displays the user's current team, allowing them to choose passive NFTs from their own collection or from other players.
- Players must form a team of 5 active NFTs and 1 passive NFT to play.
- When using other players' passive NFTs, a 5% tax cut is sent to the lender, creating passive income for them.
- Rewards depend on the weekly generated hash and the team's NFT hashes (active and passive).
- Weekly rewards are determined by comparing these NFT hashes to the generated weekly hashes.
- Players need energy to play, which replenishes daily at 12 AM GMT+8.
- Passive NFTs have specific requirements, such as needing a certain number of stars in the active NFT squad.
- Players can use other users' passive NFTs a maximum of 7 times per week.
